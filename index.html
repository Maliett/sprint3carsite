Welcome to Bournemouth Classic Car Club

Welcome to Bournemouth Classic Car Club, we are a club for classic car enthsuiasts which is open to everyone. We'd like to extend an invite for anyone to come and join our Wednesday Evening Club Nights in the Classic Car Hub. We will discuss all that is going on in the world of classic cars as well share ideas and tips and help with each others on going projects and inertests. We weclome new members with open arms, please visit our membership pages for further details. We will also feature one of our members car's as car of the month, which will be submitted to national magazines and could be featured at national level.
